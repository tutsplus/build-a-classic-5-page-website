Rates

Stay in our guest house for $120 per night, which covers you for two people. If you'd like to have extra people stay it's an additional $50 per person, per night.

Minimum stay is two nights, and we do have discounts available during the week.

We ask for a 50% deposit in advance, and if you need to cancel please let us know a week before you are due to arrive.

  • Check in time is 3pm
  • Check out is 11am
  • No smoking in the guest house
  • Please observe the 15km per hour speed limit in the complex
  • Please reduce noise after 10pm
  • Contact us to learn of any upcoming specials
